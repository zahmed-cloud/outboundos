The hard part was never the selling. The hard part is showing up every single day.

What it kills

Questions

Straight answers.

Is it really free?

Yes. It's one of the tools we built for ourselves at Ascent. We use it every day, and we made it public. You bring your own leads. Optionally add your own Claude API key for the drafting and coaching features.

Do I need to create an account?

Yes — a free one, and it takes two minutes. Email and a password, or grab an emailed login link if you prefer. Your account keeps your pipeline saved and synced, so you can pick up on any machine.

Where does my lead list live?

In your browser for speed and saved to your account, with a one-click backup file on top. Import a list and you're working it seconds later, on any machine you log into.

Does it automate LinkedIn?

No, and that's deliberate. Automation gets accounts restricted. You send every message yourself on LinkedIn; the OS tells you who, when and what to say, then logs it in one click. The default targets sit inside LinkedIn's practical limits, about 100 invites a week.

What makes it different from a normal CRM?

The truth ledger: every stat is a logged action, undo erases it everywhere, nothing double counts. Plus a daily loop with rings and a streak that makes consistency automatic, and Claude that writes in your voice, from the templates you set.
